Generative AI
AI that can create new content such as text, images, audio, video, or code based on patterns learned from data.

Computer Vision AI (Vision AI)
AI that enables machines to see, interpret, and understand images and videos, such as recognizing faces or objects.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) AI
AI that allows computers to understand, process, and generate human language in text or speech form.

Speech AI
AI that understands spoken language and converts speech to text or text to speech.

Predictive AI
AI that analyzes historical data to predict future outcomes, such as trends or behaviors.

Recommendation AI
AI that suggests products, content, or services based on user preferences and behavior.

Robotics AI
AI that controls robots to perform physical tasks by sensing the environment and making decisions.

Autonomous AI
AI that can operate independently without human intervention, such as self-driving vehicles.
